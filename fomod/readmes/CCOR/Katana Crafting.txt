===================================
Katana Crafting - CCOR Patch Notes
===================================
This file patches Katana Crafting by lautasantenni (https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/5306) for improved consistency with changes made by Complete Crafting Overhaul Remastered.  In addition to balancing the weapons against the values used in WACCF, this patch also updates the Katana Crafting's recipes with CCOR's crafting categories and conditions and adds breakdown recipes.

This is a .esp / ESL flagged file and won't take up a spot in your load order.

INSTALLATION
Both mods are required in order to use this file.
Load Katana Crafting after Complete Crafting Overhaul Remastered.

You do NOT need to use KatanaCrafting_WACCF_Patch.esp when using this CCOR patch.

CHANGES
- Includes all changes from the KatanaCrafting-WACCF Patch:
	- Changes Dwarven/Elven/Orcish stats to match WACCF weapon progression
	- Adjusts weapon stats to bring them in-line with WACCF balancing and fixes some minor inconsistencies.
- Adds crafting menu filters and category conditions from CCOR SE.
- Adds Breakdown recipes for most items.
- Adjusts the crafting ingredients to correspond to CCOR's standardized recipe formula
- Changes the name of Nord Steel, Elven, and Orcish weapons to reflect the crafting materials rather than style
