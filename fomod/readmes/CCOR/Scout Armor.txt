==============================
Scout Armor - CCOR Patch Notes
==============================
This file modifies Scout Armor SE by doodaboom, ported by DirtyWeaselMedia (https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/18910), for improved consistency with Complete Crafting Overhaul Remastered.
This is a .esp / ESL flagged file and won't take up a spot in your load order.

INSTALLATION
Both of these mods are required in order to use this file.
Load Scout Armor after Complete Crafting Overhaul Remastered.

You do NOT need to use ScoutArmor_WACCF_Patch.esp when using this CCOR patch.

CHANGES
- Includes all changes from the Scout Armor - WACCF Patch:
	-Rebalanced all items so armor is closer to Scaled and weapons are closer to Nordic in power.
	-Modified recipes and conditions to reflect the balance changes.
	-Copied over the gravity and speed stats for the Scout Arrow projectile record.
	-Added alternate recipes for Hooded Helmets (requires both a Scout Helmet and Scout Hood in inventory for recipe to show).
- Added crafting menu filters and category conditions from CCOR SE.
- Added Breakdown recipes for most items.
- Incorporated Galatite into appropriate recipes.
- Minor tweaks to a few recipe ingredients to maintain consistency with CCOR formula.
