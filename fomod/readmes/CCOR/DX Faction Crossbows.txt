====================================
Faction Crossbows - CCOR Patch Notes
====================================
This file patches Faction Crossbows by DeserterX (https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/4047?) for improved consistency with changes made by Complete Crafting Overhaul Remastered.

This is a .esp / ESL flagged file and won't take up a spot in your load order.

INSTALLATION
Both of these mods are required in order to use this file.
Load Faction Crossbows after Complete Crafting Overhaul Remastered.

CHANGES
- Updated Bolts' gravity and speed for consistency with WACCF changes.  Also carried forward fix to bolts from USSEP.
- Added crafting menu filters and category conditions from CCOR SE.
- Added conditions to reveal recipes based on either the possession of the crafting manual or the completion of initial faction quests.
