=========================================
SPOA Silver Knight Armor CCOR Patch Notes
=========================================
This file modifies SPOA Silver Knight Armor by dopalacz (https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/13236) for compatibility and consistency with Complete Crafting Overhaul Remastered.
This is a .esp / ESL flagged file and won't take up a spot in your load order.

You do NOT need to use SPOASilverKnightArmor_WACCF_Patch.esp when using this patch.

CHANGES
- Adjusted armor stats to bring them in-line with WACCF balancing.
- Added crafting menu filters and category conditions from CCOR SE.
- Added Breakdown recipes.
- Adjusted crafting ingredients slightly to better reflect CCOR's standard formula.
