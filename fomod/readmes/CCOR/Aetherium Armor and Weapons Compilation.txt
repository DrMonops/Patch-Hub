===============================================
Aetherium Armor and Weapons - CCOR Patch Notes
===============================================
This file modifies Aetherium Armor and Weapons Compilation by lautasantenni (https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/2687) for improved consistency with Complete Crafting Overhaul Remastered.

This is a .esp / ESL flagged file and won't take up a spot in your load order.

INSTALLATION
Both of these mods are required in order to use this file.
Load Aetherium Armor and Weapons after Complete Crafting Overhaul Remastered.

CHANGES
- Added crafting menu filters and category conditions from CCOR SE.
- Adjusted crafting ingredients slightly to better reflect CCOR's standard formula. 
- Changed Steel/Quicksilver ingots in recipes to Galatite.
- Added "Dwarven" to the name of the Power weapons to match the name of the armor pieces.
- Changed perk requirement from DwarvenSmithing to OrcishSmithing, and added ArcaneBlacksmith as a required perk for all recipes.
- Rebalanced armor and weapons to fit better with WACCF's balance.  Most items are now similar to Dragonbone in armor/damage rating.
