===========================================
Custom Craftable Wood Axes_CCOR Patch Notes
===========================================
Compatibility patch for Custom Craftable Wood Axes (https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/51394) by HELLJUMPERS21 and dylbill, and Complete Crafting Overhaul Remastered.
This is a .esp / ESL flagged file and won't take up a spot in your load order.

You do NOT need to use CustomCraftableWoodAxes_WACCF.esp when using this CCOR patch.

CHANGES
-Added crafting menu filters and category conditions from CCOR SE.
-Adjusted value, weight, damage speed, reach and critical of new wood axes for consistency with WACCF.
-Added appropriate keywords to new wood axes for consistency with WACCF.
-Adjusted crafting ingredients slightly to better reflect CCOR's standard formula. 
-Added breakdown recipes for most items.
-Replaced several dozen standard (iron) wood axes with new wood axes.
-Added new leveled lists to help with distribution of new wood axes.
