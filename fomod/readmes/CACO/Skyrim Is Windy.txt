CACO - Skyrim is Windy - Patch Notes

Original Author: Teabag86

Very simple patch.
Patch combines CACO Harvest script, CACO multiple ingredient harvest and Skyrim is Windy Flexible Tree Data.
Patch is a .esp/ESL Flagged file and will not take up a spot in your load order.

Recommended load order (according to Loot)
SkyrimIsWindy.esp
Complete Alchemy & Cooking Overhaul.esp
SkyrimIsWindy_CACO_Patch.esp
