==========================
Wintersun_CACO Patch Notes
==========================
This file updates Wintersun - Faiths of Skyrim to use Complete Alchemy and Cooking Overhaul's Raw Homonid Flesh instead of the vanilla Human Flesh.
Both of these mods are required in order to use this file.

Version 1.01 (23/09/2019)
-Updated for Wintersin 3.0.1
