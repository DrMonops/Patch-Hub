CACO - Rare Curios - Patch Notes

Original Author: Rusey

Applies CACO values and effects to Rare Curios' ingredients
All Rare Curios ingredient images / meshes utilised
Retains Rare Curios new effects; light and absorb magic
Adjusts LLists to distribute Rare Curios ingredients to alchemists (distribution is uncommon / rare)
Retains Rare Curios availability with Khajiit Caravans
Adds Rare Curios to CACO Formlists so they can be added to the player via the CACO MCM and all effects discovered
Patch is a .esp / ESL flagged file and won't take up a spot in your load order

Recommended load order
ccbgssse037-curios.esl
Complete Alchemy & Cooking Overhaul.esp
CACO Rare Curios Patch.esp
