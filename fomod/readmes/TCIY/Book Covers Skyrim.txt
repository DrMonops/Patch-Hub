=====================================
Book Covers Skyrim - TCIY Patch Notes
=====================================
A patch for Book Covers Skyrim by DanielCoffey (https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/901) for compatibility with The Choice is Yours.
This is a .esp / ESL flagged file and won't take up a spot in your load order.

CHANGE LOG
Version 2.2 (2021/09/12) - Updated for compatibility with The Choice is Yours v2.2.
