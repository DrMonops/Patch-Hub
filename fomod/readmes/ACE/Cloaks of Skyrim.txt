==================================
Cloaks of Skyrim - ACE Patch Notes
==================================
A compatibility patch for Cloaks of Skyrim by Nikinoodles and Nazenn (https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/6369) and Armor and Clothing Extension.
This is a .esp / ESL flagged file and won't take up a spot in your load order.

Not required (do not use) with Cloaks of Skyrim_CCOR Patch (Cloak_CCOR_Patch.esp).
