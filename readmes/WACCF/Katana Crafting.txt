===================================
Katana Crafting - WACCF Patch Notes
===================================
This file modifies Katana Crafting by lautasantenni (https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/5306) to include fixes and changes from Weapons Armor Clothing & Clutter Fixes (WACCF).

This is a .esp / ESL flagged file and won't take up a spot in your load order.

INSTALLATION
Both mods are required in order to use this file.
Load Katana Crafting after Weapons Armor Clothing & Clutter Fixes.

CHANGES
- Changes Dwarven/Elven/Orcish stats to match WACCF weapon progression
- Adjusts weapon stats to bring them in-line with WACCF balancing and fixes some minor inconsistencies.
