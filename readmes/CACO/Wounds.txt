CACO - Wounds - Patch Notes

Author: Teabag86

Applies CACO values and effects to ingredients edited by Wounds
Updates CACO recipe notes to reflect Wounds new potion effects
Replaces Wounds' Medicinal Alcohol with CACO's Distilled Alcohol
Replaces Wounds' bandages with CACO bandages
Allows use of CACO's Health poultices for wound packing
Adds new Needle and Thread recipes using CACO's spider silk and daedra silk ingredients
Adds Wounds' Needle and Thread to CACO Formlists so they can be added to the player via the CACO MCM
Merges LLists accordingly
Patch is a .esp / ESL flagged file and won't take up a spot in your load order

Recommended load order (LOOT)
Wounds.esp
Complete Alchemy & Cooking Overhaul.esp
CACO_Wounds_Patch.esp
