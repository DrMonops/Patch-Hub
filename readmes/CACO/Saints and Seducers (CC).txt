==================================================
CREATION CLUB - SAINTS & SEDUCERS_CACO Patch Notes
==================================================
Compatibility patch for Creation Club - Saints & Seducers and Complete Alchemy and Cooking Overhaul, which makes the following changes to Saints & Seducers:

-Rebalances alchemy ingredients for consistency with CACO.
-Rebalances Mystic Venom for consistency with CACO.
-Renames Green Butterfly and Purple Butterfly to "Manic Monarch Butterfly" and "Demented Swallowtail", respectively.
-Enables new mushrooms to work with CACO extra harvesting option.

This is a .esp / ESL flagged file and won't take up a spot in your load order.
