====================================
Immersive Weapons - CCOR Patch Notes
====================================
This file patches Immersive Weapons by Hothtrooper44, Ironman5000, and Eckss (https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/16788) for improved consistency with changes made by Complete Crafting Overhaul Remastered.

This is a .esp / ESL flagged file and won't take up a spot in your load order.

INSTALLATION
Both mods are required in order to use this file.
Load Immersive Weapons after Complete Crafting Overhaul Remastered.

You do NOT need to use Immersive Weapons_WACCF_Patch.esp when using this CCOR patch.


CHANGES
- Includes all changes from the Immersive Weapons - WACCF Patch:
	- Adjusts arrow stats to bring them in-line with WACCF balance and keyword changes, including gravity and speed settings.
	- Carries over WACCF's addition of the LEATHER category to the vanilla crafting menu.
	- Adjusts leveled lists to match WACCF's weapon progression.
	- Adjusts some of the weapon stats to bring them in-line with WACCF changes and fixed some minor inconsistencies.
- Adds crafting menu filters and category conditions from CCOR SE.
- Incorporates Calcinium and Galatite in a handful of recipes.
- Adjusts crafting ingredients slightly to better reflect CCOR's standard formula.
